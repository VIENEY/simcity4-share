PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

w

The Navy is back and asking to expand their naval facilities to include a new base for their submarines. Due to your excellent leadership and direction, the Navy feels your city is the ideal location to expand their operations.

These modern submarines play a crucial role in the nation's defense and deterrent forces. Giving a new meaning to the old phrase, "Silent But Deadly...", these modern marvels are virtually undetectable as they patrol the world's oceans and project your nations power & influence.

The Submarine Base is the 4th in the the Navy Base chain of conditional rewards with each subsequent base requiring just a bit more of your mayoral skills. It has features and properties similar to the first 3 Naval bases but is notably larger at 12x5 tiles in size. It is transit enabled with two entrances/gates... and vehicle automata will travel through the lot via these gates. Like at port facilities, the base is a moderate polluter. But it provides jobs to civilian workers as well as income to the city each month. It's designed to be compatible with the CDK but can be used separately.

Using the CDKN modular building methodology that is utilized by all new naval facilities, this fourth Navy Pier brings the base to life with new buildings and dockside props and personnel that shuffle & change throughout the day. The two styles of submarines will also shuffle and change berths as the deploy & return throughout the year.

Each subsequent base will require a little more mayoral effort and city development before becoming available. This base requires a city population of over 40,000, a mayor rating of 47+, Seaport usage over 80, a city that is at least 20% water (still notably less than the requirement for the Marina)... or completion of the good Fishing Boat Mission. The lot's dynamic icon info lists these requirements as well as the current levels of each requirement so you can see how close you are getting.. Once earned, the base is free to plop and will generate §400 a month for your city's coffers.

In keeping with the time honored tradition of naming vessels after notable individuals within the Community, the boats in these submarine squadrons have been christened accordingly, possibly even named after you. But this is The Silent Service, after all. So we can't tell you what they are.

INSTALLATION

From the setup .exe:
Nothing. If you are reading this, you're done.

From the zip file:
Unzip the file to your C:\My Documents\SimCity 4\Plugins folder making sure the zip "Use Folder Names" option is selected. The product will be installed in the appropriate folder under the PEGPROD sub-folder which will be created if it does not already exist on your system. If any earlier version(s) of this file exist, they should be deleted.

Once installed, the lots will be available in the menus indicated above.

DEVELOPER NOTES

This lot will plop like any CDK lot.  The lot is 5 tiles deep and the piers and ships project well out into the water... so allow room for the 'over-hang'. The ships and piers will not interfere with the path of water automata such as ferries and cargo ships... which will travel through them. Use care in selecting where you plop the base to avoid having other vessels run through the piers and ships.

Using the CDK Warehouse lot works well as a spacer between CDKN naval facilities. The lot was designed for use with the PEG CDK Industrial style and the new CDK Transition lots that blend the shallower (2 tile deep) lots with the larger (4 tile deep) CDK and CDKN (naval) lots. The lot can be plopped with either one or two rows extending into the water for greater flexibility.

* Use of the PEG CDK Leveler Tool will make the lot and the surrounding area easier to plop and use. 

NOTE -  The submarines will not appear when the lot is first plopped. Make sure the game is un-paused and allow a few months to pass. The submarines are scheduled to deploy & return and various times of the years so you will see them disappear, reappear & change berths.
