PRODUCT DESCRIPTION


 

This PEG Utopia Series lot is an improved version of the game-default Waste to Energy Power Plant. Using the real-world Plasma Gasification process to vaporize waste material, the resulting steam & SynGas are both used to power generators to produce electricity. In addition to the obvious advantage of turning trash into electricity and reusable green fuels, the process is extremely clean and environmentally friendly.


Plasma Gasification has been used for years for the disposal of industrial & bio hazard wastes. More recently, municipalities world wide are adopting the process to eliminate the huge costs and environmental issues of dumping municipal solid wastes into landfills. New technologies and innovations have greatly reduced the operating costs of this process... and have added  additional benefits in the form of generated electricity, reusable clean fuels, recyclable products and even potable water.



The Plasma Gasification Plant is a fully detailed model of a workable gasification and power generation process. The interior of the structure is illuminated to make the components visible through the dome. In-game, the lot is a identical to the game-default Waste to Energy Power Plant except that is a single tile wider (5x4) and produces very little pollution over a much smaller area.


* For more information on how the Plasma Gasification process works, please visit the related forum topic at Simpeg.Com.

INSTALLATION
All Pegasus Productions (PEGPROD) downloads are provided in ZIP archive file format for compatibility & ease of installation to all players of the game; MAC & PC alike.

The ZIP file should be downloaded and placed into your ../[Documents/My Documents]/SimCity 4/Plugins folder. When unzipped from that folder, the ZIP file will create any required new folders and place the files where they will function properly in the game and be easy for you to locate if needed.

If you encounter any issues with this procedure, check to make sure that the  "Use Folder Names" option is selected in your ZIP program.

** After installation, you can either delete the downloaded ZIP file or archive it outside of your Plugins folder structure.
DEVELOPER NOTES
This lot can be used in place of the game-default Waste to Energy Power Plant. It is an additional lot of the same type. It does not replace the default lot.

As the building model includes the raised foundation and surrounding slopped terrain, the lot should be plopped on level terrain. Plopping the lot on a slope or uneven terrain may have undesireable visual effects.

The lot requires road access for the garbage trucks. Once plopped, garbage trucks will regualrly appear around the lot.
