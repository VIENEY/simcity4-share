Lot Read Me

A biomass power plant decomposes organic matter to obtain combustible gases, like methane. The combustion of those gases is then used to produce electricity, much like a natural gas power plant.

The biomass plant produces more power than a solar power plant, at more or less the same price per MWh. However, it produces a slight amount of pollution (nowhere near that caused by a WTE plant, which works under similar principles).

This power plant is the terran version of the martian biomass power plant. When I had finished the original building for SimMars, I thought it would look cool in a standard simcity city too. So I'm releasing this one in the STEX for everyone to enjoy. For the rest of our buildings, you'll have to wait until we release a Beta :)
