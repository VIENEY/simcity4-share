B.A.T. Model by Somy.
Modding by Equinox.

Functionally similar to the microwave plant of SC3K, this power plant harnesses the power of the sun's ultraviolet rays and concentrates them into a beam of incredible energy, which is then converted to useable electricity in the core of the facility, deep underground. Due to the workings of this giant magnifying glass, there is a quite severe fire danger! Make sure you have a well-funded fire station nearby to prevent it from catching fire (if it does, and it's not extinguished quickly, it will blow up). If you keep it funded well, it has an expected lifespan of approximately 1,400 years (the high plop cost pays for this longevity).

Looking at the statistics, keep in mind that this lot's stats were balanced with the game's default power stations. For example, this plant provides more than twice the amount of power the hydrogen plant does monthly, costs slightly less than twice its monthly cost, and has a far longer lifespan.

Note about Custom Model Nightlighting Effects
 

In order to see night lighting, you need to have the EP1 Update 1 for Rush Hour/Deluxe installed, and then afterwards install the patch specifically for the BAT nightlighting, available on the "Building Architect Tool" page on the official Maxis website.  It is a separate download because it is optional and would add 19 MB to the other update. Also, you must be logged into the Maxis website to see the download link.

If you do not have this installed, no custom model you
download will display any lighting effects at night.
