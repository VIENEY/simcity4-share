PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Pegadyne Industries is pleased to announce the opening of the their new Oceanographic Institute off the coast of your city. This long-awaited private university will take advanced education to the next level offering masters & doctorate programs in Oceanography, Marine Biology, Marine Engineering & HydroSimology. City planners anticipate a large increase in High Tech industrial demand as graduates from this elite institution hit the job market.

The Oceanographic Institute is an alternative to the game's university and has the same properties and characteristics. But unlike the university's massive size, the institute only takes up one, single land tile. The entire facility resides completely in the water.

INSTALLATION

If installing from the ZIP archive, unzip the file to your C:\My Documents\SimCity 4\Plugins folder making sure the zip "Use Folder Names" option is selected. The product will be installed in the appropriate folder under the PEGPROD sub-folder which will be created if it does not already exist on your system. If any earlier version(s) of this file exist, they should be deleted.

Once installed, the lot will be available in the menu(s) listed above in the Products Details section.

DEVELOPER NOTES

This lot must be plopped in the water with the end of the projecting bridge on land. Rotate the lot when plopping it until the bridge projection is visible... then position the end of the bridge on the edge of the shore. The lot will turn blue when properly placed.

The land around the end of the bridge will conform and lower to the bridge's fixed height in the same manner as the game's seaport. The lot can be plopped in any depth of water but may look better in shallower water. The lot contains details below sea-level that might not be visible in deeper water.

The bridge to the lot is transit enabled and should be connected with a road to your existing traffic network.
