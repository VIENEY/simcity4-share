Lot Title Beaches for Irregular Coasts
Author CryinG.FreeM4n
Version 3.2 (2004/03/31)



Outline

  • No lot of this package of beaches replaces the original beach of the game.
  • This pack contains 10 new lots, based on original beach properties.
  • To improve performances, none of these new lots, except one, has the seagull effect.
  • Now, a transit-enabled version is available.



Installation Instructions

  • Unzip the files.
  • Copy one of the .dat files to your C:\My Documents\SimCity 4\Plugins folder.
  • (Fm_BeachesForIrregularCoasts_TD is the transit-disabled version)
  • (Fm_BeachesForIrregularCoasts_TE is the transit-enabled version)



Description

These beaches are perfect for the irregular coasts of your cities.
  • Beach (Standard) is the common beach lot.
  • Beach (Seagulls) is the same lot as above with seagulls.
  • Beach (Large) represents 2 Beaches (Standard) one beside the other.
  • Beach (Link) is the lot designed to intercalate itself in the small spaces formed by the other lots.
  • Beach (Land Corner) is the lot designed for the corners of coasts open on the land.
  • Beach (Sea Corner) is the lot designed for the corners of coasts open on the sea.
  • Beach (North-East Oriented) is the lot designed for diagonal coasts directed towards the North-East (lot system of reference).
  • Beach (North-West Oriented) is the lot designed for the alternative case.
  • Beach (Underwater Sand) is used to extend the beaches in the sea if you see the limit of the other lots.
  • Beach (No Parking) is the same lot as Beach (Standard) except the parking area was removed, it enables you to place parks or RCI zones.
