PRODUCT DESCRIPTION


 
The CDK3-SP Row Warehouses is a collection of 5 lots that are designed for use with the CDK3-SP Seaport series. Styled along the lines of older break-bulk freight warehouses that string along the docks of many harbors, these lots are all modular and designed to join together to form warehouses of any length and can even wrap around corners.

Available in 2x, 3x, 4x,  Inner & Outer Corner varieties, the lots can be used by themselves or linked together to wrap around any terrain. The lots can also be plopped fully on dry land for use along your railways or part of a larger rail-freight facility.

All of the lots are ploppable. All are IND-M lots with jobs, except for the Outer Corner lot which can be difficult to provide road access to. All of the lots are fully compatible with other CDK3-SP lots and can be used anywhere within your CDK3-SP harbor project.


The Inner Corner & 4x lots are transit enabled for rail. The other lots are not and therefor can easily be plopped next to each other. The 3x lot features a transit-enabled central driveway that is visually enabled only. It will allow a connection to an adjacent roadway but will not impair traffic flow in anyway.

All of the lots feature a multitude of  random, timed props that appear and disappear throughout the day to simulate activity. The randomness of the props should allow you to plop numerous lots together without it appearing too repeatitive.

Again, sorry for the rather long list of dependencies but as stated before, if you are using these lots with your other CDK3-SP  Seaport lots, you should already have all of these files installed.

INSTALLATION
If installing from the ZIP archive, download the file to your C:\My Documents\SimCity 4\Plugins folder. Then, unzip the file making sure that the "Use Folder Names" option is selected. The product will be installed in an appropriate folder under the PEGPROD sub-folder which will be created if it does not already exist on your system. Once installed, the product will be ready to use in your game. Lots will appear in the menu listed in the above Product Details section.
** After installation, you can either delete the downloaded ZIP file or archive it outside of your Plugins folder satructure.
DEVELOPER NOTES

These lots are CDK3 lots and designed to be plopped over water with the inner most 3 rows of tiles plopped fully on dry land.  Unlike most other CDK3 lots, these lots can also be plopped fully on dry land for use inland as rail-freight facilities, etc...

The 3x lot has a driveway entrance that is transit enabled to make a visual connection only. The lot itself is not transit enabled. It will connect automatically to an existing road network.  If the road network is added after the lot is plopped, you can connect it manually using streets. This connection is only visual. It is not required for lot functionality. However, the lot must have road access in order to function properly as it has jobs..


The rail running through the middle of the Inner Corner & 4x lots is fully transit enabled for both passenger and freight rail. This was added to give the player the option of connecting the lots to the city's rail network and/or allowing functional rail to pass through the lots. However, the rail is not used by the lot itself in any functional way. For most applications, it can be ignored completely. The rail on all other lots is not transit enbaled in any way.


You can not plop the Inner Corner & 4x lots adjacent to each other because of the rail transit enabling. Use the other, non transit enabled lots as filler lots. You can also use any other CDK3-SP lot to act as spacers between sections of warehouses, etc...

For coastal use, these lots must be plopped with the 3 inner rows of tile on dry land. It is often helpful to use the CDK Leveler tool before plopping CDK lots.
