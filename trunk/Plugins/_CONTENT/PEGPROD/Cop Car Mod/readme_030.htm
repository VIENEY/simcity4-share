PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Tired of those old & clunky looking cop cars? What bad 1950s movie did they find those in? Well that's all about to change with the new PEG Police Car Replacement Modd.

This pack offers you a choice of 10 new modern police car styles that replace the game's automata as well all the props around the police stations. Its all automatic... just install the style of your choice and all the police cars in the game change to that style. There's even a new static prop in each style thrown in for the use of developers who want a cop on-lot 24/7.

The available styles include:

* 4 styles of Black & White units with varied markings:
- Star on doors.
- Municipal seal on doors.
- Sheriff's Unit.
- SimCop's police cruiser... dedicated to Officer SimCop who is the TopCop in our books... and a major contributing factor for the success of the KrispyKremes franchise.

* 5 styles of all White units with varied markings:
- Standard ' POLICE ' cruiser.
- White w/ Blue stripe.
- White w/ Orange stripe.
- NYPD
- St. Lucie County Sheriff's Department in Florida. Dedicated to Deputy Dan1083 who is serving with pride through thick & thin... not to mention several hurricanes.

* The official S.C.P.D. police cruiser... a "powerfully" blue & white unit that stands out and lets your Sims know that SimCity's finest is on the job.

INSTALLATION

If installing from the ZIP archive, unzip the file to your C:\My Documents\SimCity 4\Plugins folder making sure the 'Include Folder Names' option is selected. The product will be installed in the appropriate folder under the PEGPROD sub-folder which will be created if it does not already exist on your system. If any earlier version(s) of this file exist, they should be deleted.

Once installed, the contents of this development pack will available to any lot that references them and will also be available for use in the Lot Editor.

If installed normally, all 10 of the replacement files will be in an appropriate folder in your Plugins folder. YOU MUST REMOVE ALL THE REPLACEMENT FILES EXCEPT THE ONE YOU WISH TO USE. ONLY ONE FILE CAN BE INSTALLED AT A TIME.

FOR DEVELOPERS...
Each style file contains exemplars that circumvent the game's default cop car props, including the 6 time varied props that are used to create the shuffling effect in the default police station lots. In the Lot Editor, these props will show up normally in the props list and you can use them to develop your own lots. In the Lot Editor, you will see the style that you have installed.

When using your lot, other players will see the style they have installed... or the default props if they do not have this mod installed. Either way... you're covered and can freely develop with this mod and not worry about dependencies.
